It is a program we hope to expand from the current thirty-three beneficiaries as funds become more available to benefit other needy members of the society.The Educational Scholarship Scheme is aimed at funding the education of the less privileged in society. We shall partner with various willing private sector stakeholders as well as institutions of learning from primary to tertiary level to create an avenue whereby the corporate responsibilities of the organizations could be leveraged for the benefit of identified indigent students.The Motherless Babies Welfare Initiative, as the name implies, caters for the welfare of the almost forgotten members of the larger society. It is our goal to make sure that they are not left behind in the scheme of things as pertain to quality of life and social integration. We want to ensure that they are nurtured in a warm and caring environment devoid of systemic neglect and abuse by partnering with various government and non-governmental agencies charged with their welfare to promote better opportunities for a brighter future.The Civil Service Merit Award Program is geared towards the fundamentals of individualâ€™s desire for Acknowledgement, Celebration and Encouragement.
